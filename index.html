About the Program

Cricket that connects, moves and heals.

Active Wyndham is Wyndham City Council's community health initiative. The cricket program uses the sport as a powerful tool to improve physical health, mental wellbeing, social connection and community belonging across the whole Wyndham region.

  • ✓Backed by Wyndham City Council and Cricket Victoria — real organisations, real support
  • ✓Programs for all ages — kids 6+, youth, adults, seniors & people with disabilities
  • ✓Sessions at multiple reserves across Point Cook, Werribee & Hoppers Crossing
  • ✓Part of the Active Wyndham Strategy 2026–2031 — a Council commitment to making Wyndham active for everyone
  • ✓$3.75 million invested by Wyndham City in cricket facilities, wickets, nets & pavilion upgrades
Our Programs

Find the right program for you

From school holiday fun to competitive all-abilities cricket — there's a home for everyone in Wyndham's cricket community.

🏫
Active Holidays — Come & Try Cricket
Free school holiday cricket sessions run with Point Cook Centrals CC and West Point CC. Kids learn to throw, catch, bat and bowl in a fun, inclusive environment. No experience needed — all abilities catered for.
Ages 6–17 Point Cook & Werribee FREE
🦁
Wyndham Tigers — All Abilities Cricket
One of Victoria's most celebrated inclusive clubs — over 30 years of cricket for people with intellectual disabilities. Summer cricket season runs October to February in the MAACA competition. New players always welcome. Contact Greig Donald: 0409 257 199.
Teens–Adults Warringa Crescent Reserve, Hoppers Crossing All Abilities
⭐
Woolworths Cricket Blast
Cricket Australia's official introductory program for young kids, delivered through Point Cook Centrals and West Point Cricket Club. A perfect first step into cricket for boys and girls aged 5–10.
Ages 5–10 Point Cook Introductory
🏆
Point Cook Centrals Cricket Club
A warm, community-oriented club with boys and girls teams welcoming players of all ages and abilities. Develop your skills and compete in local competitions — Featherbrook Oval is home base.
All Ages Featherbrook Oval, Point Cook Community Club
💚
West Point Cricket Club
A thriving community club partnering directly with Active Wyndham and Cricket Victoria to deliver holiday programs and pathway cricket for juniors in Point Cook. [email protected]
Junior–Senior Point Cook Community Club

Heart Health & CPR at Cricket
Cricket Victoria has partnered with Staying Alive Health to bring life-saving defibrillators and CPR training to community cricket clubs across Victoria — including Wyndham. Every player and spectator is safer for it.

Backed By

Supported by real organisations

The Active Wyndham cricket program is fully supported by government, sporting bodies and dedicated community clubs.

🏛️

Wyndham City Council

Delivering the Active Wyndham Strategy 2026–2031 to support all residents in reaching health and wellbeing goals. Sport & Recreation: 1300 023 411

🏏

Cricket Victoria

The state governing body providing coaching resources, pathways, CPR programs and defibrillators to community cricket clubs statewide.

💰

$3.75M Sport Revitalisation

Wyndham City has committed $3.75M to upgrading local sporting facilities — including cricket wickets, nets and pavilions — across 2025–2027.

❤️

Staying Alive Health

Partnered with Cricket Victoria to install defibrillators and deliver CPR training at cricket clubs across Victoria — protecting every player and spectator.

How to Get Involved

Three easy steps

1

Find Your Program

Browse the Active Wyndham Calendar at wyndham.vic.gov.au/activeholidays or scroll up to find the right program for your age and interest.

2

Register Online

All Active Wyndham registrations are through Humanitix — free, quick and secure. Or contact the club or Council directly.

3

Show Up & Play

Bring water and sunscreen. Equipment is provided. All ages, levels and abilities are welcome. Just show up and have a great time!

Ready to play? Come join us.

No experience. No cost. No barriers. Just great sport and great people across Wyndham, Victoria.
